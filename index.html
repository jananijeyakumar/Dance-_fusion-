About The Academy

Sixteen Dance Traditions, One Family

Dance Fusion began as a single studio with one mirror and a dream — to give every dancer a stage worthy of their passion. Today we train students of every age across classical, Latin, street, and folk traditions.

  • Internationally certified trainers
  • Sprung-floor studios for safe practice
  • Annual showcases & competition teams
